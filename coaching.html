Team Officials

Coach: Kelley Kozel (Simplicity Horsemanship LLC)

Teaches weekly lessons at Simplicity Horsemanship, provides feedback at shows, and develops personalized training plans for riders at all levels. Kelley's dressage expertise and horsemanship knowledge create a supportive learning environment where every rider can grow and succeed.

President: Julia Jordan

Leads meetings, coordinates with IDA and university officials, and oversees all club functions and decisions.

Vice President: Kiara Jones

Assists the president, manages team schedules, and steps in to support any club initiative.

Treasurer & Communications Chair: Alex Awtrey

Collects dues, manages the budget, runs social media accounts, and keeps members updated on club news.

Social Chair: Pauline Cho

Plans team dinners, bonding activities, and social events that bring members together off the horse.

Fundraising Chair: Lindsey Ruffin

Organizes fundraisers, sells raffle tickets, and secures resources to offset member costs.

Secretary: Vee Hobart

Records meeting minutes, tracks attendance points, and maintains the official club roster.
