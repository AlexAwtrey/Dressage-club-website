Team Officials

Coach: Kelley Kozel (Simplicity Horsemanship LLC)

Kelley is a dedicated dressage professional with a deep passion for equestrian training and horse well-being. With expertise in classical dressage and horse care, she plays a vital role in ensuring that our members receive expert guidance tailored to their equestrian journey. Her commitment to excellence and holistic approach makes her an invaluable part of our team.

President: Julia Jordan

As president of Dressage Club at Auburn, Julia brings leadership and passion for equestrian sports. Her role involves overseeing all club functions, presiding over meetings, and serving as the main contact for IDA and the university. Julia's dedication helps create a supportive and thriving team environment.

Vice President: Kiara Jones

Kiara assists the president, manages scheduling, and supports officer activities. Her organizational skills and dedication to the team help ensure smooth operations and successful events throughout the season.

Treasurer & Communications Chair: Alex Awtrey

Alex manages the club's finances, collects dues, and oversees accounts while also handling social media, announcements, and communications. Her dual role ensures both financial stability and strong communication within the team.

Social Chair: Pauline Cho

Pauline organizes bonding and service events to promote team spirit. Her creativity and enthusiasm help foster a vibrant equestrian community and create memorable experiences for all members.

Fundraising Chair: Lindsey Ruffin

Lindsey organizes fundraisers each semester to support club activities and offset costs for members. Her dedication to securing resources helps make participation more accessible and ensures the club has the funding needed for shows, equipment, and travel.

Secretary: Vee Hobart

Vee maintains the club roster, keeps meeting minutes, and manages attendance and club records. Her organizational skills ensure smooth communication and accurate documentation of all club activities and decisions.
