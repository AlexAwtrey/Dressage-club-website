Member Information

How to Join

Joining the Auburn Dressage Club is easy! Simply contact us through our Contact page or attend one of our meetings at the beginning of the semester. We hold informational meetings during the first few weeks of each semester.

Membership Requirements

  • Must be currently enrolled at Auburn University
  • Complete membership form and liability waiver
  • Pay semester dues of [INSERT AMOUNT]
  • Attend orientation session (for new members)

What Membership Includes

  • Weekly riding lessons with Coach Kelley
  • Access to club horses and facilities
  • Eligibility to compete at IDA shows
  • Team apparel and gear (available for purchase)
  • Social events and team bonding activities
  • Educational workshops on horse care and riding technique

Member Expectations

  • Attend regular practices and meetings
  • Maintain good academic standing
  • Contribute positively to the team environment
  • Follow all barn rules and safety protocols
  • Treat horses, the coach, and fellow members with respect
  • Support teammates at shows and events
